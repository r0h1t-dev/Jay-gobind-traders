About Us

Building trust and delivering excellence since 2012 AD

A Legacy of Trust & Quality

Jay Gobind Traders is a family-owned business established in 2012 by Yugal Kishor Chaudhary and Rakesh Kumar Chaudhary. Starting from a small trading unit in Nepal, we have grown into a trusted supplier specializing in coal import, brick industry supply, agriculture materials, and goods transportation. Our strong presence in cross-border trade with India allows us to deliver reliable and high-quality products across Nepal.

With a solid network and a commitment to quality, fair pricing, and timely delivery, we proudly serve brick factories throughout the Kathmandu Valley and other regions of Nepal. Over the years, our dedication to transparency, customer satisfaction, and dependable logistics has helped us build long-term relationships and establish ourselves as a reliable partner in Nepal's industrial and agricultural sectors.
